Gong cha is a popular Taiwanese tea shop chain founded in 2006 with a focus on high-quality, personalized tea experiences. The name "Gong cha" means "to provide the best tea to the emperor" in Chinese, reflecting the brand's commitment to excellence. Gong cha now has thousands of locations worldwide, offering a wide variety of customizable milk teas, fruit teas, and specialty beverages made with fresh, premium ingredients. Known for positive word-of-mouth and unique, tailored service, Gong cha aims to elevate traditional Taiwanese tea culture through modern flavors and trends.
